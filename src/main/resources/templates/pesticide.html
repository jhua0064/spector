3. Check Weather Condition If you Still Choose to Spray Pesticide

 
Current Temperature: ° C

Current Wind Speed: km/h

Before using any pesticides, it’s always recommended to read the instructions that you need to follow. Most of the product descriptions describes about the weather conditions, droplet size, equipment and spray drift resistance as well that will help the users.

Before you start, check that the weather conditions are suitable for spraying (ie wind speeds between 3–15 km/h, blowing away from sensitive crops/areas, Delta T between two and eight, temperature below 28°C, no inversion layer present). If the weather is unstable or unpredictable, don’t spray. Continue to monitor weather conditions while spraying and stop spraying if it turns unfavourable. Learn More
